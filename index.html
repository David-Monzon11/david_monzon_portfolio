Design Process

A user-centered, iterative approach.

  1. 01

    Research

    Understand the problem, users, and context through stakeholder interviews, audits, and user research.

    Define goals, constraints, and success metrics to keep everyone aligned.

  2. 02

    Wireframe

    Explore flows and information architecture with low-fidelity sketches and wireframes.

    Validate concepts early with teams and users before investing in visuals.

  3. 03

    Design

    Translate ideas into polished, accessible interfaces with a strong visual hierarchy.

    Build reusable components and patterns that scale.

  4. 04

    Develop

    Implement designs in code with attention to performance, semantics, and responsiveness.

    Collaborate closely with engineering for smooth handoff and iteration.

  5. 05

    Test & Iterate

    Validate with real users, track behavior, and refine the experience over time.

    A/B tests, usability sessions, and analytics guide meaningful improvements.
